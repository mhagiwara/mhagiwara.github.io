Research internship opportunity at Rakuten Institute of Technology, New York (Deadline: March 8, 2014)

Rakuten Institute of Technology, New York (RIT-NY) is the research division of Rakuten Inc., one of the largest e-commerce companies in the world. We are looking for motivated and talented students for summer research internship in New York.

Internship information is the following:

Research topic examples (not limited to the following):

  • Fundamental language analysis (tokenization, part-of-speech tagging, named entity recognition, parsing) on e-commerce text
  • Machine translation and multilingual processing of e-commerce text
  • Lexical knowledge acquisition & dictionary/ontology construction for e-commerce language processing
  • Information extraction, sentiment analysis, and other applications using noisy, huge e-commerce data to improve user experience

Rakuten is rapidly expanding globaly, and the data from Rakuten group companies all over the world may be used for the research, including Rakuten Ichiba (Japan), Rakuten Taiwan, Rakuten.com Shopping (US), Rakuten Linkshare (US), PriceMinister (France), Rakuten.de (Germany), and so on.

This is a research internship position. Successful interns are encouraged to apply state-of-the-art technologies to the business, and to publish their work at the end of the internship.

Required skills:

  • PhD or MSc students in Computer Science or related disciplines (If you are MSc students, we prefer people who are planning to pursue a PhD)
  • Strong programming skills on Linux (no preference on programming languages)
  • Strong English communication skills (just as reference, TOEIC 700 or more is preferred)
  • We will accept around 2 people from world-wide, no limitation or preference based on nationality or region

Preferred skills:

  • Experience in machine translation and language analysis
  • Fluency in one or more of the following languages: German, Spanish, French, Indonesian, Portuguese, Thai, Chinese
  • Experience in machine learning and big data processing (MapReduce)

Condition:

  • Term: any three-month period within the window of June to October 2014 (three-month period is preferable, but we can negotiate on this matter)
  • Salary, return trip to New York and accommodation (for non-New York residents) will be provided
