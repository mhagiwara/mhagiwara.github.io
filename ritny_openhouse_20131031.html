Invitation: Open House at Rakuten Institute of Technology (Oct 31st, 2013)

Rakuten Institute of Technology, New York (RIT-NY) is the research division of Rakuten Inc., one of the largest e-commerce companies in the world. We are happy to announce that we are hosting an Open House session at RIT-NY on Oct 31st, from 2:30pm, to introduce our research topics. Students, professionals in academia, and those who work in related fields are warmly welcomed

At the beginning of the open house, Prof. Satoshi Sekine, the Director of RIT-NY, will introduce the institute and our research topics. This will be followed by a poster booth session, presented individually by our researchers and interns

Our research topics include:

  • Novel user experience design for e-commerce applications
  • Developing technologies using noisy Big Data to improve usability and/or accuracy for e-commerce applications
  • Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing for e-commerce applications

Full-time positions and internship opportunities will be available, so you are encouraged to attend if you are interested in applying.

Snacks and refreshments will be served!
